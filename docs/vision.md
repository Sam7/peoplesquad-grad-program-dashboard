This dashboard is PeopleSquad’s **student-first “map of the grad program landscape”**: a single place where students can quickly browse the top graduate employers in Australia and see—using only official sources—the few facts that actually drive good decisions (eligibility, streams, key dates, hiring stages, and what each employer signals they value). For students, it turns a messy, time-consuming, anxiety-inducing research task into a fast, confident shortlist and a clear next step (“am I eligible, is it open, what do they look for, and where do I apply?”), while also giving light context on what the company is focused on right now so they can tailor how they present themselves. For PeopleSquad, it’s a trust engine: by providing transparent, genuinely useful guidance upfront (without gatekeeping), we become the default guide students return to—naturally increasing brand familiarity, credibility, and course conversion without needing aggressive marketing.
