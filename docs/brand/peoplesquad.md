## **Values/Approach**

### General

* People Squad values authenticity, pragmatism and good humour. We’re Australian but with an urban, global perspective.  
* Our unique perspective comes from being on the other side of the hiring desk in the corporate world where we’ve seen people make lots of mistakes.   
* We’ve also worked across Higher Education and high schools where we encountered the hopes and dreams of students and their parents but also the perspectives of employers.   
* We don't believe that work is the only thing that matters or that it can solve all of your problems.   
* We’re focused on providing online resources (courses and software) that can empower individuals and institutions at scale \- we don't want to become face-to-face providers  
* We value and partner with those offering, rich in-person coaching or guidance \- careers advisers in schools/unis, private career coaches etc. We want to form long-term partnerships where we can support institutional careers services but also refer people to trusted advisers/coaches (and have them refer clients back to us)  
* We especially don’t want to become or veer into the territory of life coaches or gurus.   
* We want to be bold but we’re mindful of broken promises and liability \- we don’t want to guarantee employment outcomes, higher wages etc. So much depends on the circumstances of an individual and broader forces in the labour market.

### Artificial Intelligence (AI)

#### Detailed statement about AI use

**Authentic Insights, Enhanced by Tech** At People Squad, we use AI tools to help structure complex career concepts into clear, memorable forms and to develop visual resources like infographics. While technology helps us **organise** information, the expertise remains strictly professional. Every piece of content is vetted by veteran hiring experts, and all instructional videos and interviews feature real people with deep industry experience. We use AI to sharpen the delivery, but the wisdom comes directly from the source.

#### TLDR statement about AI use

We use AI to **organise** concepts and design infographics, but every lesson is vetted by real hiring experts and delivered by real humans (not avatars\!). **Authentic wisdom, tech-enhanced delivery.**
